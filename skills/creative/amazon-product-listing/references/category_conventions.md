# Category-Specific Conventions

Different product categories have different visual conventions that customers expect. Matching these conventions builds trust; breaking them makes a listing feel off even when nothing is technically wrong.

Load this file after identifying the product category in Step 1 of the workflow.

## Beverages (bottles, cans, pouches, cartons)

**Main image:**
- Label facing camera, slightly angled (10–15°) for depth
- Show full product from cap/top to bottom
- No condensation on main image (implies chilling, which is a context)

**Secondary image ideas:**
- Liquid pour shot (mid-action, into a glass)
- Ice / condensation lifestyle shot
- Ingredient origins (fruit, grain, water source)
- Usage moment (drinking, pairing with food)
- Flavor variants lined up together
- Nutritional highlights infographic

**Lifestyle context:** Kitchen, outdoor café, gym, morning routine, with food.

## Food (packaged goods, snacks, pantry items)

**Main image:**
- Package front forward, standing upright
- Show the full package shape
- Avoid showing the product outside the package on main (that's secondary territory)

**Secondary image ideas:**
- Open package revealing product
- Product on a plate / served / plated
- Recipe suggestion with product
- Ingredient callouts
- Nutritional infographic
- Serving size / what's in the package
- Flavor variants

**Lifestyle context:** Kitchen counter, dining table, picnic, with family, meal prep.

## Skincare / Cosmetics

**Main image:**
- Package front + cap on
- Show brand/product name clearly
- Minimal shadow, clean studio look

**Secondary image ideas:**
- Texture close-up (cream swatch, serum drop, lipstick bullet)
- Application demonstration (model applying to face/skin)
- Key ingredient highlights (hero ingredient with benefit)
- Before/after (if legally supportable)
- Packaging detail (pump, dropper, magnetic closure)
- Variant shades / full product line

**Lifestyle context:** Bathroom with natural light, vanity, morning/evening routine, model's face close-up.

## Electronics & Gadgets

**Main image:**
- Front view or 45° angle showing main interface
- Screen on with a clean, neutral display (avoid trademarked content)
- Show key buttons/ports without making them dominant

**Secondary image ideas:**
- Port/button detail shots
- Size comparison (vs. phone or hand)
- In-use setup (connected, on desk, mounted)
- Specifications infographic
- What's in the box (device + cables + manual + adapter)
- Compatibility chart (works with: iPhone, Android, Windows, etc.)
- Feature callouts (IP rating, battery life, wireless range)

**Lifestyle context:** Home office, desk setup, travel scene, commuting, gaming setup.

## Apparel

**Main image:**
- **Live model standing** OR **invisible mannequin / ghost mannequin** OR **flat lay**
- NEVER traditional mannequin
- Model must be standing, not sitting/lying/leaning
- Pure white background

**Secondary image ideas:**
- Fabric / material close-up (texture, weave)
- Detail shots (stitching, buttons, zippers, labels)
- Fit photos (front, back, side on model)
- Size chart / measurement diagram
- Color variants in flat lay grid
- Lifestyle outfit (styled with other clothing)
- Care instructions infographic

**Lifestyle context:** Bedroom, outdoor stroll, casual social scene, city street, park.

## Footwear

**Main image:**
- Side profile OR 3/4 angle
- Single shoe OR pair (both shoes)
- Laces neatly tied
- Pure white background

**Secondary image ideas:**
- Sole tread detail
- Upper material close-up
- Top-down view
- Comfort features (cushioning, insole, lining)
- Size reference (next to ruler or common object)
- In-use lifestyle (walking, hiking, running, standing)

## Home Goods / Kitchenware

**Main image:**
- Product in natural upright position
- Show the most recognizable silhouette
- White background, centered

**Secondary image ideas:**
- In-use scene (pan on stove, blender on counter)
- Size dimensions with measurement lines
- Material/finish detail
- Cleaning/care shot
- What's included (with all accessories)
- Feature callouts (dishwasher safe, induction compatible, BPA-free)

**Lifestyle context:** Kitchen in use, dining setup, cooking scene.

## Furniture

**Main image:**
- Product at natural angle (usually 3/4 view for dimension)
- Pure white background
- Include any included cushions/pillows as part of the product

**Secondary image ideas:**
- Dimensions diagram with measurements
- Detail shots (fabric, stitching, wood grain, hardware)
- In-room styled scene (with complementary furniture, rugs, lighting)
- Assembly preview
- Weight capacity / durability specs
- Color/material variants

**Lifestyle context:** Styled living room, bedroom, home office scene.

## Fitness / Sports Equipment

**Main image:**
- Full product clearly visible
- Pure white background
- For apparel: model standing

**Secondary image ideas:**
- In-use shot (person exercising with the product)
- Technology / material features
- Size / weight specs
- Compatibility (fits standard racks, dimensions, etc.)
- Variant sizes or colors
- Included accessories

**Lifestyle context:** Home gym, outdoor run, yoga studio, athletic scene.

## Pet Products

**Main image:**
- Product alone, pure white background
- No pet on the main image (pets are props; forbidden on main)

**Secondary image ideas:**
- Happy, healthy animal USING the product (pet IS allowed on secondary)
- Size reference with pet of known size
- Material/safety features (non-toxic, BPA-free)
- Cleaning / care instructions
- Variant sizes (small, medium, large)

**Lifestyle context:** Home with pet, outdoor walk, pet using product naturally.

## Toys & Kids Products

**Main image:**
- Product alone, pure white background
- No child on main image

**Secondary image ideas:**
- Age-appropriate child playing safely with the product
- Educational/developmental benefits
- Safety certifications (ASTM, CPSIA compliant — if real)
- What's in the box (all pieces)
- Size/weight
- Variants (characters, colors, sizes)

**Safety note:** For toy products, any child in lifestyle images should appear age-appropriate for the product's age rating, using it safely.

## Books

**Main image:**
- Front cover only, perfectly rectangular
- No perspective distortion
- High-resolution, all text readable
- Pure white background

**Secondary image ideas:**
- Back cover with synopsis
- Spine detail
- Sample interior pages (respecting copyright)
- Author info
- Series/collection family shot

## Beauty Tools (hair dryers, curlers, brushes)

**Main image:**
- Product at 45° angle
- Any cords neatly coiled or minimal
- Pure white background

**Secondary image ideas:**
- Heat / technology specifications
- In-use shot (styling demonstration)
- Attachments included
- Size/weight comparison
- Travel-friendly features
- Result photos (hair styled with the tool)

## Jewelry

**Main image:**
- Macro detail showing the piece clearly
- Pure white background
- Include any chain/clasp that's part of the product

**Secondary image ideas:**
- Worn on model (ear, neck, wrist, finger)
- Size reference (next to coin or on a finger)
- Material certifications (14k gold, sterling silver, sourced diamonds)
- Gift packaging (if included)
- Variants (chain lengths, stone options)

## Category-specific selling point priorities

| Category | Top selling points to highlight |
|----------|--------------------------------|
| Beverages | Ingredients, sugar content, natural, flavor, caffeine level |
| Food | Ingredients, certifications (organic/non-GMO), flavor, portion |
| Skincare | Active ingredients, skin type, concerns addressed, certifications |
| Electronics | Specs, compatibility, battery life, warranty |
| Apparel | Material, fit, care, size range |
| Footwear | Material, comfort tech, sole type, width options |
| Home goods | Material, care, warranty, dishwasher/microwave safe |
| Furniture | Materials, dimensions, weight capacity, assembly |
| Fitness | Material, weight capacity, dimensions, durability |
| Pet | Material safety, size range, cleaning, durability |
| Toys | Age range, safety certs, educational value, battery-free |
| Beauty tools | Heat range, technology, attachments, cord length |
| Jewelry | Material purity, stone details, chain length, gift-ready |
